Welcome to the Hand Gesture Racing Game!

This game is controlled by hand gestures.
There will be a video in the bottom left that will show your hands being detected.
In order to start the game, you must press the start button in the center of the screen.
In order to steer you turn your hands like a steering wheel. Your thumbs must be up.
If you want to speed up or slow down, you can move your hands closer and further away from the camera.
In order to zero your player, you must move to the location you want and put your thumbs down.
When you are ready to drive again, put your thumbs back up.
Make sure to look at the map below before you begin!
